# Mod Explorer

Browse the full mod.io catalog for Insurgency: Sandstorm without leaving the panel.

There's a search bar at the top — type anything and results filter as you go (with a small debounce so it doesn't hammer the API). Next to it is a sort dropdown: Trending, Most Downloaded, Most Subscribed, Recently Updated, or Highest Rated.

Each mod shows up as a card with its image, name, description snippet, download/subscriber/rating stats, and tags. Two buttons per card:

- **Subscribe** — adds the mod to your current profile
- **External link** — opens the mod.io page in a new tab

Results are paginated with Previous/Next buttons at the bottom. Shows the current page, total pages, and total result count.
